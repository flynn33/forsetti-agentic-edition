# Constitution

> Canonical source: [`FORSETTI_CONSTITUTION.md`](../FORSETTI_CONSTITUTION.md)

Summary of the Forsetti Agentic Edition Constitution — the highest governing document in the repository.

## Authority Hierarchy

The Constitution establishes a binding precedence order for all governance documents:
1. Constitution (highest)
2. Policy documents
3. Standards
4. Contract templates
5. Machine-readable policy manifests
6. Role-specific instructions
7. Task contracts
8. Issue/PR/workflow-local instructions

## Foundational Principles

The Constitution defines seven foundational principles: Contract Before Action, Scope Is Binding, Truthfulness Is Mandatory, Governance Overrides Convenience, Documentation Is Part of Delivery, Compliance Must Be Measurable, and Release Integrity Is Non-Negotiable.

## Constitutional Protections

Certain elements are immutable without a formal constitutional amendment, including the foundational principles, authority hierarchy, role doctrine, and the requirement for task contracts and compliance evidence.

## Amendment Process

Constitutional amendments require governance-class approval, must be proposed as standalone governance changes, and must include rationale, downstream impact assessment, and rollback plan.
